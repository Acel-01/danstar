Unleash your Creativity

This app would help you showcase all the dance moves embedded within your mind, the flips, the grind, the spin and all moves you wish your body would just let you do and be awesome.


1. Create Profile

Place your name and details into our system for us to keep a record of your dance.

2. Create an Avatar

Create a unique avatar to use in all your dances, be it to just share with friends or for your keeps.

3. Create Scenes

You can create the places you find yourself dancing in, every scene created in your head can be reproduced. Never stop imagining.

4. Select Music

With over 200 songs, search for the one that keeps singing in your head. Feel free to download more

5. Dance Dance Dance

Now, you can create the magical dance in your head, go on dance battles and Just have fun.

6. Share your creations

Show off your masterpiece amongst your peers. Let them see that you can also rock it on the dance floor and be on point.
